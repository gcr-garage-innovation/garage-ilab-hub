Step 1：启动设备Step 1: Start the workstation

  1. 打开 I-Lab 指定设备并登录。Power on the assigned I-Lab workstation and sign in.
  2. 确认网络、输入法、浏览器可正常使用。Confirm network, input method, and browser are working.
  3. 如需帮助，请联系现场支持人员。If needed, contact on-site support.

Step 2：使用模型与数据处理Step 2: Use models and process data

  1. 按指引打开模型/工具并开始任务。Open the model/tools as instructed and start your task.
  2. 你的文件可临时保存在本地 D 盘。You may temporarily save files to local drive D:.
  3. 如需保留成果，请在离开前自行拷贝带走（U 盘/个人合规存储位置）。If you need to keep outputs, copy them out before leaving (USB or approved personal storage).
重要提醒：Important:
  • 设备重启后会执行环境还原。Environment reset is applied after reboot.
  • 未带走的数据将无法恢复。Data not copied out cannot be recovered.
  • 请勿在设备中长期留存敏感信息。Do not keep sensitive data on the workstation.

Step 3：结束与离开Step 3: Finish and leave

  1. 关闭使用中的工具和文档。Close all tools and documents in use.
  2. 检查 D 盘文件是否已完成拷贝。Verify files in D: have been copied out.
  3. 退出账号并离开工位。Sign out and leave the workstation.

Step 4：完成调查问卷Step 4: Complete the survey

  1. 扫码/点击链接填写 Survey。Scan QR code or open the survey link.
  2. 反馈本次使用体验、问题和建议。Share your experience, issues, and suggestions.
  3. 提交后即完成本次 I-Lab 流程。Submission completes this I-Lab session.

员工完成标准Completion criteria

  • 已完成一次模型实操。Completed one hands-on model task.
  • 已拷贝并带走所需成果。Copied and retained required outputs.
  • 已提交调查问卷。Submitted the survey.
